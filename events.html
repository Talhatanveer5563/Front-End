Merciado Events

Find theme park celebrations, sporting events, festivals, concerts, cultural happenings, outdoor festivities, consumer trade shows and even virtual experiences in Orlando with Visit Orlando's Calendar of Events. Discover fun for everyone, including Orlando's world-famous holiday celebrations such as Easter, Halloween, Thanksgiving and Christmas, as well as Visit Orlando's Magical Dining® in August. Also, be sure to visit our list of Orlando hotels and resorts to find your perfect accommodations during your stay, and sign up for our eNewsletter for the latest information about Orlando events you won’t want to miss. Just like Orlando’s theme parks, attractions, hotels, restaurants and other businesses, many of our event organizers have rolled out enhanced health and safety procedures that include face-mask and social-distancing requirements, increased sanitization, and other measures. It’s all part of our community effort to keep you protected while you’re having the time of your life in the Theme Park Capital of the World!


Conecrts & Live Music


Merciado knows how to put on a great show! Every year, we attract a broad range of talent from all over the world, including superstars from rock, country, Latin, EDM, R&B and just about every other genre imaginable. Festival & Annual Events

No matter when you visit, chances are you’ll find fun festivals in Orlando. Between mouthwatering food and beverage fests, nationally ranked art shows, and acclaimed celebrations of film, theater and music — not to mention our theme parks’ famous events — there’s something for just about every taste, too. And don’t worry about the timing of your visit. Thanks to Merciado.’s year-round temperate climate, we never let Mother Nature stop us from hosting festivals you won’t want to miss! Many of our festival organizers have also joined with our theme parks, attractions, resorts, restaurants and other businesses in employing enhanced health and safety measures designed to protect you while you’re here. As a result, you can get out and enjoy festivals with confidence in Merciado!
